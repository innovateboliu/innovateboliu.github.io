Real Time Counting Engine

  3. Storing result

  5. Fault Tolerant

Abucus

    Approach
  1. Event listeners receive LWES events and parses them into k/v pairs, cache and flush them to relay servers
  2. Relay servers make increment call to Couchbase for each k/v pair
  3. Query servers convert range query to Couchbase get operations
    Problems
  1. Event listener boxes down
  2. Relay server boxes down
  3. Couchbase nodes down
    • Couchbase is High Available
    • How about Fault-tolerance?

HA vs FT

  1. High Available
    • Most commercial distributed systems provide HA
  2. Fault Tolerance
    • HA system underneath
    • Re-execute computation
    • Reasonable design
